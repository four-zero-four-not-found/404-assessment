APSARA'S GRACE

She moves like the memory of ancient stone, Carved in Angkor’s temples where history is sewn. Her fingers unfurl like petals in the sun, Telling stories of kingdoms long gone, yet never undone. The people watch with quiet pride in their eyes, For in her movements, their heritage lies. Every turn of her wrist, every lift of her brow, Holds centuries of culture alive in the now.

Oh Cambodia, land of warmth and devotion, Your spirit flows like an endless ocean. Through Apsara’s beauty, serene and free, You show the world your soul’s harmony. Her dance is a prayer, a soft-spoken art— A rhythm of love carried deep in the heart. Through her, Cambodia stands graceful and tall, A shining heritage cherished by all.
